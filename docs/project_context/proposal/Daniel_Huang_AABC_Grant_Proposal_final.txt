Planet-Typing  Near  the  Radius  Valley:  Can  Sub-Neptunes  Masquerade  as  Terrestrial  Worlds  in  
Reflected-Light
 
Spectroscopy?
  Abstract:  The  Habitable  Worlds  Observatory  (HWO)  aims  to  directly  image  and  spectrally  characterize  
Earth-sized
 
planets.

in

⊕

by

envelopes
 
(Fulton

2017;
 
Rogers

Lopez

In

  3.  Methodology  3.1  Sub-Neptune  forward  modeling  with  PICASO:   We  will  use  PICASO,  an  open-source  reflected-light  radiative  transfer  model  capable  of  arbitrary  phase  
geometry
 
(Batalha

type.
 ●  Metallicity:  up  to  ~10³×  solar,  motivated  by  sub-Neptune  composition  diversity  and  by  the  strong

 ●  Wavelength  range:  compute  spectra  over  the  full  reflected-light  range  typically  used  in  PICASO
